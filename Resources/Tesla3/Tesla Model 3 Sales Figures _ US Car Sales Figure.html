How many Model 3 did Tesla sell in 2022?

Tesla sold 240,266 Model 3 in 2022. In 2021, Tesla sold 121,610 Model 3. They sold 118,656 more units in 2022 compared to 2021 with a percent gain of 97.57% YoY.
