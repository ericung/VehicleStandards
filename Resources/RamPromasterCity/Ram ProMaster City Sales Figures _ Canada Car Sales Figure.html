Key Stats
How many ProMaster City did Ram sell in Q4 2021?

Ram sold 191 ProMaster City in Q4 2021.

How many ProMaster City did Ram sell in 2021?

Ram sold 521 ProMaster City in 2021. In 2020, Ram sold 558 ProMaster City. They sold 37 more units in year 2020 compared to 2021 with a percent loss of 6.63% YoY.

Overview

The 2020 Ram ProMaster City is a vehicle from automaker Ram.

How Much Does the 2020 Ram ProMaster City Cost?

Pricing for the 2020 Ram ProMaster City starts at $32,970 MSRP and goes up to $33,970 fully loaded.

Ram ProMaster City Engine, Transmission, and Performance Numbers

Ram ProMaster City is powered by a front-wheel-drive powertrain that makes 178 horsepower and 174 ft-lb torque.

2020 Ram ProMaster City Fuel Economy

The 2020 Ram ProMaster City has a rating of 8 L/100 km city, 8 L/100 km highway. The 2020 Ram ProMaster City can hold 61 liters of fuel for a maximum range of kilometers.

List of Ram ProMaster City Competitors

Some of Ram ProMaster City competitors include the Ford Transit Connect.
