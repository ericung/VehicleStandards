Key Stats
How many Ram Pickup did Ram sell in 2023?

Ram sold 223,050 Ram Pickup in 2023. (From Jan - Oct 2023)

How many Ram Pickup did Ram sell in Q2 2023?

Ram sold 117,700 Ram Pickup in Q2 2023.

How many Ram Pickup did Ram sell in 2022?

Ram sold 468,344 Ram Pickup in 2022. In 2021, Ram sold 569,388 Ram Pickup. They sold 101,044 more units in year 2021 compared to 2022 with a percent loss of 17.75% YoY.

Overview

The 2024 RAM 1500 is a vehicle from automaker Ram.

How Much Does the 2024 RAM 1500 Cost?

Pricing for the 2024 RAM 1500 starts at $37,920 MSRP and goes up to $68,460 fully loaded.

RAM 1500 Engine, Transmission, and Performance Numbers

RAM 1500 is powered by a 5.7-liter engine with a Rear Wheel Drive powertrain that makes 395 -hp and 269 lb-ft torque. The transmission on the RAM 1500 is an 8-Speed Automatic (850RE) (STD).
